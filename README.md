Risk is defined as:

1. level of risk to natural disasters (lower is safer)
    - number and severity of different natural disasters. disasters are defined only in terms of:
        - property damage
        - injuries
        - casualties
2. volatility of temperature / temperature change (lower is safer)
3. air quality (higher is better)
4. proximity to coastlines

Will be using Random Forest Refgressor to produce continuous safeness values, and to develop continous location data.

Will connect it to heatmap with cartopy.
